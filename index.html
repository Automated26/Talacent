Our story

BUILT SLOWLY, ON PURPOSE.

Talacent was built without outside investment, without volume targets, and without taking on mandates we didn't believe we could honour. That remains true today.

What started as two people with a clear point of view is now a two-office firm with a growing list of retained clients. The ambition is clear. The principle has never changed.

— Section 01

FOUR PRINCIPLES. HELD CLOSELY.

Values are easy to write and hard to live. These four are the ones we audit ourselves against, quarterly, without exception.

— 01

CONVICTION OVER CONVENIENCE

We won't present a shortlist we don't believe in. If the right person isn't in the room yet, we keep looking. Full stop.

— 02

CANDID COUNSEL

Our clients pay for judgement, not agreement. That means telling you when the role is scoped wrong or the package won't land.

— 03

LONG RELATIONSHIPS

We measure success in the years after placement, not the weeks before it. Our repeat client rate is the metric we care about most.

— 04

DISCRETION, WITHOUT COMPROMISE

Executive search requires sitting with confidences. We treat that trust as the foundation of the firm. It is why we don't publish client lists.

— Section 02

Every engagement is led by a partner. No account managers, no handoffs. The person who wins your trust is the person who does the work.

— Section 01

HOW A SEARCH UNFOLDS.

Every engagement follows the same four-stage cadence. We vary the timeline, not the rigour.

01

BRIEF & CALIBRATE

Two to three working sessions to map the role, context, and cultural brief. Written position spec before anything else.

02

RESEARCH & MAP

Full market map covering every plausible candidate, including those who aren't looking. Prioritised against your calibration.

03

ENGAGE & ASSESS

Candidates approached discreetly and assessed through structured interviews and reference triangulation.

04

PLACE & SUSTAIN

Offer management, transition, and a 12-month integration review. The relationship doesn't end at signature.

01

PRIVACY POLICY.

How Talacent collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: January 2026

1. About This Policy

Talacent Pty Ltd (ABN TBC) ("Talacent", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This policy explains how we collect, hold, use, and disclose your personal information.

2. What Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact details including name, email address, phone number, and company name
  • Professional information including employment history, qualifications, and career objectives
  • Information submitted through our contact form or chatbot (Mia)
  • Website usage data collected via Google Analytics (anonymised)
  • Correspondence and communications between you and Talacent

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide recruitment and talent advisory services
  • Respond to your enquiries and manage your engagement with Talacent
  • Match candidates with relevant executive roles
  • Communicate with you about relevant opportunities or services
  • Improve our website and services through anonymised analytics
  • Comply with our legal and regulatory obligations

4. Disclosure of Information

We will not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may disclose your information to prospective employers or clients only with your express consent, and to service providers who assist us in operating our business (such as Formspree for form processing and Google for analytics), subject to confidentiality obligations.

5. Chatbot Data (Mia)

Our AI assistant Mia is powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Conversations with Mia may be processed by Anthropic in accordance with their privacy policy. We collect conversation data to qualify enquiries and book discovery calls. Booking details are sent to info@talacent.com. We do not retain chat logs beyond the active session.

6. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects anonymised data about your visit, including pages viewed and time spent. This data does not identify you personally. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access. All form data is transmitted via HTTPS. We review our security practices regularly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@talacent.com. We will respond within 30 days.
